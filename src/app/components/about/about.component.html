About us

At New Day Communications, we're passionate about helping you achieve your goals and grow your business. We work with you to understand your challenges and provide customized solutions that suit your budget and timeline. That is why we offer a full range of complimentary services that help you connect with your customers, employees, partners, and stakeholders.

New Day Communications is a leading provider of mobile content solutions for businesses and customers in the mobile telecommunications industry. We operate in the value-added service sectors in Kenya and aim to expand our services across the African continent

We help businesses connect with their customers through our gateway platform that delivers content via mobile. Our platform also enables businesses to understand their customer segments, usage, and consumer behaviours. With our expertise and experience in the value-added service business, we can handle the complexities of mobile operator integrations.

Our services in Kenya are powered by our dynamic platform that supports all bearers offered by mobile operators. This means that we can offer you a complete solution for all your mobile integration needs.

Whether you need Digital Content, Training, Business Process Documentation & Implementation, Product Development, Marketing and Management, Customer Value Management (CVM), Customer Experience Service, or Business Consultancy, we have you covered - because we believe that communication is the key to success.
